GOOD TO KNOW

🎫 Visitor Pass
Each guest is charged a $10 visitor pass fee per session. When booking online, this fee is included for drop-ins, clinics, lessons, and court rentals for the booking guest. Additional guests pay the $10 fee upon arrival.
❄️ Snowbird Membership Pause
Members can pause their membership at any time, given 30 days written notice. Members on pause will be charged a $10 monthly fee but will not be charged a new initiation fee when returning.
🚪 Cancellation Policy
Members can cancel their membership at any time, without penalty, given 30 days written notice.
