Our Story

BUILT BY PLAYERS,
FOR PLAYERS

Wayne and Lisa Dollard — husband and wife and former collegiate tennis players — discovered pickleball in 2015 before it was really a "thing." They went on to found Pickleball Magazine, now in its 10th year, before turning their attention to building the finest indoor pickleball facility in the country right here in the south suburbs of Pittsburgh.

"We incorporated ideas from the best clubs around the country and added our own wish list of amenities." The result is LevelUp — 16 courts with Category 1 broadcast-quality lighting, a 7-layer US Open cushioned surface found at fewer than 1% of courts nationwide, a full bar and restaurant, speakeasy, golf simulator, pro shop, and ballroom.

Over the years the Dollards built LevelUp Pickleball Camps into a national platform now operating at 280 locations per year across 43 states — developing champions and helping everyday players level up their game.
