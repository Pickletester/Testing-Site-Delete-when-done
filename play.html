LevelUp offers a wide range of play for everyone to enjoy — from casual drop-in to competitive leagues and tournaments.

WAYS TO PLAY

Daily Drop-in Play
Drop-in times segmented for Beginner (2.0–2.9), Intermediate (3.0–3.9) and Advanced (4.0+) players. Show up at the designated morning and evening "drop-in" times and you'll be paired with random players of similar skill level.
