Questions?

FREQUENTLY ASKED
QUESTIONS

Where is LevelUp Pickleball Club?
▼
Our facility is located at 33 Mayview Road in Canonsburg, across from Henderson-Hills Elementary School. We have 128 parking spots on site and plenty of off-site parking as well.
What are your hours?
▼
We are open 7:00 a.m. to 10:00 p.m., 7 days per week.
How do I become a member?
▼
Visit our membership page and view our membership plans. We have options for $29, $124, and $149 per month, as well as discounts for seniors, military, and first responders.
How do I book a court?
▼
Our website features CourtReserve software. You can book courts through our Member Portal at app.courtreserve.com. Members get advance booking windows depending on their tier — Diamond gets 2 weeks advance, Gold gets 1 week, and Silver gets 2 days.
Can I rent courts?
▼
Members can play during morning and evening drop-in times. If members choose to book private courts, they are available for $25 per hour during non-prime times or $40 during prime times.
Do I have to have a membership to play?
▼
Non-members are welcome to participate in drop-in times and reserve courts during prime times. However, non-members will not be permitted to play in leagues, ladders, most social events, or many tournaments.
Do you offer lessons?
▼
When it comes to instruction, we have the nation's premier instructors right here! Wayne and Lisa Dollard are the 50+ US Open Mixed Champions and Doubles Champions, publishers of the national Pickleball Magazine (325,000 circulation), and owners of the sport's largest teaching organization — LevelUp Pickleball Camps.
What if I am a beginner?
▼
We welcome players of all levels! If you have never played before, we encourage you to register for our Pickleball 101 session. Our pros will have you competing and having fun in no time.
What if I don't have anyone to play with?
▼
When you sign up for a time to play at LevelUp, we will take care of the rest. While we encourage you to invite friends and family, we will guide you to an open court based on your skill level to play with others. You will be part of an entire community in no time at all.
Do you offer leagues?
▼
We do! Opening Summer 2025. Visit our leagues tab — if there are open registrations available, you can read more and sign up for the league that makes sense for you. Only one player registers per team.
